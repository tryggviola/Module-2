The big mac is a hamburger sold by international fast food resaurant Chain mcdonalds. It was introduced in the greater pittsburgh area in 1967 and nationwide in 1968. It is one of the companys flagship Products and signature dishes. The big mac was created by jim Delligatti, an early ray kroc franchisee, who was opperating several Restraunts in the pittsburgh area. It was invented in the kitchen of Delligatti´s first mcdonalds franchise, located on mcknight road in Suburban ross township.

Coca cola is a carbonated soft drink manufactured by the coca-cola company. Originally marketed as a temperance drink and intended as a patent medicine, it was invented in the late 19th century by jogn stith pemverton. The drink name refers to two of its original ingredients: coca leaves and kola nuts.
